<div class="z-help"><h1>Adoptium 软件仓库</h1>
<h2>Windows/macOS 用户</h2>
<p>通过以下链接打开下载页面，选择所需的版本，下载独立安装包。</p>
<div class="z-wrap"><form class="z-form" onchange="form_update(event)" onsubmit="return false"></form><pre class="z-code"></pre></div><tmpl>
{{endpoint}}
</tmpl>
<h2>Debian/Ubuntu 用户</h2>
<p>首先请安装依赖：</p>
<div class="z-wrap"><form class="z-form" onchange="form_update(event)" onsubmit="return false"></form><pre class="z-code"></pre></div><tmpl z-lang="bash">
{{sudo}}apt-get update &amp;&amp; {{sudo}}apt-get install -y wget apt-transport-https
</tmpl>
<p>然后信任 GPG 公钥：</p>
<div class="z-wrap"><form class="z-form" onchange="form_update(event)" onsubmit="return false"></form><pre class="z-code"></pre></div><tmpl z-lang="bash">
wget -O - https://packages.adoptium.net/artifactory/api/gpg/key/public | {{sudo}}tee /etc/apt/keyrings/adoptium.asc
</tmpl>
<p>随后将下列内容添加到 <code>/etc/apt/sources.list.d/adoptium.list</code> ：</p>
<div class="z-wrap"><form class="z-form" onchange="form_update(event)" onsubmit="return false"><div><label for="b4020f72" title>操作系统版本</label><select id="b4020f72" name="release" title><option value="bookworm">Debian 12 (bookworm)</option><option value="bullseye">Debian 11 (bullseye)</option><option value="buster">Debian 10 (buster)</option><option value="stretch">Debian 9 (stretch) (EOL)</option><option value="jammy">Ubuntu 22.04 LTS (jammy)</option><option value="focal">Ubuntu 20.04 LTS (focal)</option><option value="bionic">Ubuntu 18.04 LTS (bionic)</option><option value="xenial">Ubuntu 16.04 LTS (xenial) (EOL)</option></select></div></form><pre class="z-code"></pre></div><tmpl z-input="release" z-path="/etc/apt/sources.list.d/adoptium.list">
deb [signed-by=/etc/apt/keyrings/adoptium.asc] {{endpoint}}/deb {{release}} main
</tmpl>
<p>再执行</p>
<div class="z-wrap"><form class="z-form" onchange="form_update(event)" onsubmit="return false"></form><pre class="z-code"></pre></div><tmpl z-lang="bash">
{{sudo}}apt-get update
</tmpl>
<p>之后可以安装软件包，例如：</p>
<div class="z-wrap"><form class="z-form" onchange="form_update(event)" onsubmit="return false"></form><pre class="z-code"></pre></div><tmpl z-lang="bash">
{{sudo}}apt-get install temurin-17-jdk
</tmpl>
<h2>CentOS/RHEL/Fedora 用户</h2>
<p>将下列内容添加到 <code>/etc/yum.repos.d/adoptium.repo</code> ：</p>
<div class="z-wrap"><form class="z-form" onchange="form_update(event)" onsubmit="return false"></form><pre class="z-code"></pre></div><tmpl z-lang="ini" z-path="/etc/yum.repos.d/adoptium.repo">
[Adoptium]
name=Adoptium
baseurl={{endpoint}}/rpm/centos$releasever-$basearch/
enabled=1
gpgcheck=1
gpgkey=https://packages.adoptium.net/artifactory/api/gpg/key/public
</tmpl>
<p>再执行</p>
<div class="z-wrap"><form class="z-form" onchange="form_update(event)" onsubmit="return false"></form><pre class="z-code"></pre></div><tmpl z-lang="bash">
{{sudo}}yum makecache
</tmpl>
<p>之后可以安装软件包，例如：</p>
<div class="z-wrap"><form class="z-form" onchange="form_update(event)" onsubmit="return false"></form><pre class="z-code"></pre></div><tmpl z-lang="bash">
{{sudo}}yum install temurin-17-jdk
